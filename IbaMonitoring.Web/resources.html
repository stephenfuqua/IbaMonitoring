Resources

The methods, protocols, and pilot season outcomes are described in the following publication:

  • Homayoun, T. Z. and R. B. Blair. 2009. Citizen-Science Monitoring of Landbirds in the Mississippi River Twin Cities Important Bird Area. Pages 607-616 in T.D., Rich, C. Arizmendi, D. Demarest and C. Thompson, editors. Tundra to Tropics: Connecting Birds, Habitats and People. Proceedings of the 4th International Partners in Flight Conference, 13-16 February 2008, McAllen, TX. Partners in Flight.

References used in developing the statistical results:

  • Bibby, C. J., Burgess, N. D., and Hill, D. A. 1992. Bird Census Techniques. Academic Press Limited, London.
  • Magurran, A. E. 1988. Ecological Diversity and Its Measurement. Princeton University Press, Princeton, NJ.
